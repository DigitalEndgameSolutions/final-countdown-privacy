Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

Final Countdown ("the App") is developed by Rupert Russell. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you create in the App (event names, dates, notes, and photos) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Photos

If you choose to add a custom photo icon to an event, the image is selected via Apple's PhotosUI framework and stored locally on your device. The App does not access your full photo library — only the specific image you select is used, and it never leaves your device.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. No data is shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, it is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
